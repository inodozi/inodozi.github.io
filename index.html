About

Hello! I am Iman, a Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at UC Santa Cruz. My research interests are Stochastic Control, Hybrid Systems, and Convex Optimization. I am working under the supervision of Abhishek Halder.

In my free time, I go hiking, watch movies, learn to play piano, and go running.
